Graduate Internship Application Process

Application Steps:
  • 1. Sign up for an account on this page. (top right corner)
    Please make sure you have your student email and student ID ready.
  • 2. Fill in an internship application
    You will need the information on offer letter to complete the application
  • 3. schedule a meeting with academic advisor to confirm academic qualification
    Schedule an appointment
  • 4. Your internship coordinator will review your application and approve it
    Internship coordinator is assigned based on the internship course you selected.
  • 5. Dean will review and approve your application
    Internship coordinator is assigned based on the internship course you selected.
  • 6. After Dean sends your application to Registar, you will see the internship course on your workday.
